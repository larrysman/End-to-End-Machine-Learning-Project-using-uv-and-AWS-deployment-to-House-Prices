## `END-TO-END MACHINE LEARNING PROJECT USING UV WITH DVC, MFLOW AND DEPLOYMENT TO AWS`

### `PROBLEM STATEMENT`

**Why Hosuing Price Prediction Matters**
- Real Estate markets require accurate pricing models for buyers, sellers, and investors to make informed decisions in a volatile market.

### `DATASET & SCOPE`
- The United States Housing Dataset with geographic, demographic, and economic features spanning multiple years of the market data.

- Link to the US Housing Dataset: `https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shengkunwang/housets-dataset`
- Link to the US Metro Map Area Dataset: `https://simplemaps.com/data/us-metros`

### `PROJECT GOAL`
**To build a production-ready system with automated pipelines, monitoring, and scalable cloud infrastructure to predict the housing prices in the United States of America.**
